Rent In Crypto

Rent In Crypto is a cryptocurrency powered Platform/Ecosystem, designed to easily facilitate rent payments between tenants and landlords in the form of digital cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and RentDex.

Various incentives will be provided to use our ERC20 utility token in the ecosystem, including discounts on rent, yield farming and more.
